# 11 - Modalities Survey

## Scope

- Extend the map beyond plain text generation at survey depth first.
- Focus on which latency metrics, bottlenecks, and serving constraints transfer beyond LLMs and which do not.

## Notes

Use `notes/` for embeddings, VLMs, ASR, TTS, image, and video serving caveats.

## Experiments

Use `experiments/` for small modality-specific benchmarks or serving prototypes.

## Exit Criteria

- At least two or three modalities are surveyed with concrete bottleneck notes.
- You can explain what transfers from LLM inference and what does not.
